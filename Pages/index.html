Pomodoro Technique



The POMODORO TECHNIQUE is a time manegment method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980's.It uses a timer to break work into intervels,traditionally 25 minutues length,seperated by short break.Each intervals is known as a POMODORO,from the Italian word for a tomoto,after the tomoto-shaped kitchen timer Cirillo used as a university student.
